The Purpose and Reasoning



This website is about looking at how social media has evolved in many ways and not only with online interaction, with family, friends and collegues, but it has also created a new way of shopping, we are seeing how the rise of online shopping and online business have flooded the internet as well as social media platforms. we will be looking at how many online stores have flooded social media platforms to conduct their sales, before and during the pandemic,especially in South Africa and in the USA. we are also as well as the new tool, interfaces and features that these platforms have developed to increase user interactivity on their platforms. basically we are looking at online business that conduct their sales using social media plaforms, that were tradtionally used for instant messages and personal updates.

Using a business API and the social media states(of how many business profiles and shops were opened in the past 5 years) to calculate the online shopping data.
